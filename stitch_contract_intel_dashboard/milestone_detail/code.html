Foundational Pour Phase 1

Accepted
AMOUNT $150,000.00
PERCENTAGE 10%

Work Items

Site preparation and excavation grading to specified depths.
Installation of rebar framework per structural drawings B-01.
Pouring of high-strength concrete mix (C-30) for foundational slab.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Concrete slump test must fall within 4-6 inches upon delivery.
  • Rebar layout must pass independent inspection by structural engineer prior to pour.
  • Core samples at 7 days must indicate a minimum compressive strength of 2200psi.

Payment Conditions

Payment for the Foundational Pour Phase 1 is strictly contingent upon meeting all physical acceptance criteria and successful submission of QA/QC documentation. The Contractor must provide certified test results from an approved third-party laboratory.

Crucially, concrete must reach 3000psi before payment is triggered. Invoices submitted prior to achieving the 28-day 3000psi benchmark will be held in abeyance without incurring interest or penalty to the Owner.
