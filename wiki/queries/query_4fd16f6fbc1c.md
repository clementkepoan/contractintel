# whats the second milestone of the project?

## Answer
The second milestone of the project is when the乙方完成設備交貨(即附件五報價單各細項工程「設備交貨項目」之勾選項目)經甲方及客戶確認后。According to Clause [C4] (第六條 付款辦法), the payment for this milestone will be 30% of the total contract price, which is 5,008,500 TWD.
