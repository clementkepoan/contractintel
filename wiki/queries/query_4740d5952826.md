## 結論
This contract is a **fixed-price lump-sum engineering contract**, with payment structured based on milestone achievements tied to both vendor’s performance and client’s payments. The total contract amount includes tax.

## 各期付款
- **第1期**：3,339,000 TWD (20% of total contract); request condition: 乙方提交細部設計圖經甲方及客戶核定
- **第2期**：5,008,500 TWD (30% of total contract); request condition: 設備交貨並經甲方及客戶確認
- **第3期**：5,008,500 TWD (30% of total contract); request condition: 軟體及硬體設備安裝完成經甲方及客戶核定
- **第4期**：3,339,000 TWD (20% of total contract); request condition: 工程全部驗收合格（甲方及客戶）

All payments are conditioned on甲方收到當期客戶之工程款。乙方須開具請領發票及相關文件，甲方於收到確認後至 **45天**內付款。
